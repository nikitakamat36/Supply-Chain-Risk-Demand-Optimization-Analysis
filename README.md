# Supply-chain-Analysis
End-to-end retail analytics project analyzing sales, inventory, and stockout impact using SQL and Power BI Supply chain risk analysis project identifying revenue loss due to stockouts and demand inefficiencies Retail data analysis project focused on demand forecasting and inventory optimization

#Problem Statement:

Retail businesses often face revenue loss due to stockouts and inefficient demand planning.
This project analyzes sales, inventory risk, and customer behavior to identify demand patterns and improve supply chain decisions.

#Tech Stack

1.SQL (Data Analysis)

2.Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

3.Excel (Data Cleaning)

#Key Metrics

1. Total Revenue: ₹15M

2. Total Transactions: 5000

3. Average Order Value: ₹3052

4. Stockout Rate: ~52%


#Key Insights

1. Electronics category generates the highest revenue

2. TV is the top-performing product

3. Sales peak in August, indicating seasonal demand

4. Loyal customers contribute the majority of revenue

5. Promotions provide moderate impact on sales


#Critical Finding

High stockout rates are causing significant revenue loss despite strong product demand.

#Business Impact

1. Identified high-demand products facing frequent stockouts

2. Highlighted revenue loss due to inventory shortages

3. Provided insights for demand forecasting and inventory planning

#Recommendations

1. Implement demand forecasting for seasonal planning

2. Increase inventory for high-demand products

3. Optimize promotion strategies

4. Improve supply chain efficiency in key locations
